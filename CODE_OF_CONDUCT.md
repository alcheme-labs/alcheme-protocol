# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We want Alcheme Protocol to be a respectful, technically rigorous, and welcoming project.

Participants are expected to:

- communicate with respect, even during disagreement
- assume good intent while staying precise about technical concerns
- give feedback that is actionable and specific
- avoid harassment, personal attacks, discrimination, or intimidation

## Unacceptable Behavior

This includes, but is not limited to:

- abusive or demeaning language
- targeted harassment
- doxxing or publishing private information
- threats, intimidation, or sustained hostility
- bad-faith review behavior intended to block or exhaust contributors

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject content that violates this code of conduct. Repeated or severe violations may lead to temporary or permanent participation restrictions.

For conduct concerns that should not be discussed in public, do not post sensitive details in a public thread. Ask a maintainer to establish a private reporting path before sharing private information.
